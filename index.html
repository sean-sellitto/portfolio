Projects I'm proud of

Access

McGraw Hill

Introducing McGraw Hill's Access App, a cutting-edge learning tool designed to enhance your study experience. This personalized and interactive solution streamlines the process of studying, making it more efficient than ever before. With the Access App, users gain access to a vast collection of trusted medical and allied health content. Whether you prefer videos, articles, or other mediums, this app has you covered. It caters to your unique learning needs, ensuring you have the resources you need at your fingertips. During my time with the Access App team, I specialized in testing the Android application. Working closely with the iOS and Middleware teams, I collaborated to eliminate any potential testing gaps between platforms. Additionally, I championed a shift left testing approach, bringing the team to the forefront of efficient testing methodologies. I'm proud to share that the Access App has been recognized as a Webby Honoree, further validating its exceptional quality and impact in the digital realm.

Technologies used include:

  • Kotlin
  • Espresso
  • Wiremock
